Brief introduction

Association of Muslim Doctors

AMD is a collection of likeminded people for a common cause. Islam came as a blessing for all mankind (Al-Quran 21:107) and it is duty of all Muslims to exhibit this very basic tenant in the deeds and action of their daily life. This was the background in which some young Muslim Doctors of Bihar decided to form a registered body in its inaugural meeting at Hajipur on 20thFeb 2009. About 35 Doctors attended under the banner of Islamic Medical Association of India (IMAI) by the initiation of Dr.Md Athar Ansari Convener with steering committee lead by Founder Secretary Dr. Abbas Mustafa with an idea to organise Muslim Doctors in the form of an association which laid down its very foundation on moral values and basic teachings of Islam
